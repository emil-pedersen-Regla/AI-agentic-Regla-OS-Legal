### Conflict of Interest Policy

**Identification**:
- All board members and senior management must disclose conflicts
- Annual conflict of interest questionnaire
- Disclosure at beginning of each board meeting

**Management**:
- Conflicted person recuses from discussion and vote
- Decision made by disinterested directors/shareholders
- Transaction must be fair to company
- Document conflict and resolution in minutes

**Related Party Transactions**:
- Must be on arm's length terms
- Require board approval (by disinterested directors)
- May require shareholder approval if material
- Document rationale and fairness opinion

## Governance Best Practices

### Board Effectiveness
- Regular board meetings with structured agendas
- Distribution of materials in advance (5-7 days ideal)
- Adequate time for discussion and questions
- Executive sessions (board without management) when appropriate
- Annual board self-evaluation
- Director education on industry, regulations, fiduciary duties

### Transparency and Communication
- Regular financial reporting to board and shareholders
- Prompt disclosure of material developments
- Clear communication of strategy and progress
- Management accessibility to board members
- Proper documentation of all significant decisions

### Compliance and Risk Management
- Establish compliance calendar for regulatory obligations
- Regular legal and regulatory compliance review
- Risk register identifying key risks and mitigations
- Insurance coverage review (D&O, E&O, general liability)
- Whistleblower policy (if applicable)
- Data protection and privacy compliance (GDPR)
