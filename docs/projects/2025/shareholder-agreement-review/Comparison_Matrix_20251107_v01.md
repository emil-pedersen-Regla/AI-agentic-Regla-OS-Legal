## Decision-Making Comparison

### FA Decision Framework:
- **Majority vote** for major decisions
- **CEO tiebreaker** in deadlock
- **2/3 override** of CEO if needed

**Example Outcomes:**
| Vote Split | FA Result |
|------------|-----------|
| 2-1 (CEO in majority) | CEO position wins |
| 2-1 (CEO in minority) | Majority wins |
| 1-1-1 | CEO breaks tie |
| 3-0 against CEO | CEO overridden |

### SA Decision Framework:
- **Unanimity** required for material decisions
- **No tiebreaker** mechanism
- **Any party veto** blocks decision

**Example Outcomes:**
| Vote Split | SA Result |
|------------|-----------|
| 2-1 (any direction) | DEADLOCK |
| 1-1-1 | DEADLOCK |
| 3-0 | Passes |

### Material Decisions Requiring Unanimity (SA Clause 6.1):
1. Articles amendments
2. Dividends
3. Merger/IPO/listing
4. Liquidation/bankruptcy
5. Debt >50k DKK (~â‚¬6,700)
6. Loans/guarantees >50k DKK
7. Asset/IP sales
8. Expenses >50k DKK (one-off)
9. **Hiring ANY employee**
