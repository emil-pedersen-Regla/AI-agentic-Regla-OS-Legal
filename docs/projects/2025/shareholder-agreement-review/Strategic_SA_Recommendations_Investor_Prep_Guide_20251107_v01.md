# PART I: INVESTOR PSYCHOLOGY & MESSAGING

## Understanding Investor Concerns

### What Investors Fear

**Fear #1: Founder Lack of Commitment**
- **Concern**: "Will founders stick around long enough to build value?"
- **Evidence They Look For**: Vesting schedules, cliff periods, acceleration terms
- **What Triggers Fear**: No vesting, short vesting periods, immediate 100% ownership

**Fear #2: Misaligned Incentives**
- **Concern**: "Do founders want to build big company or cash out early?"
- **Evidence They Look For**: Liquidation preference structure, exit provisions
- **What Triggers Fear**: Participating preferences (founder wants early exit), low drag-along thresholds

**Fear #3: Operational Chaos**
- **Concern**: "Can this team execute? Will founder disputes paralyze the company?"
- **Evidence They Look For**: Governance structure, decision-making mechanisms, deadlock provisions
- **What Triggers Fear**: Unanimous consent requirements, no tiebreaker mechanisms, all founders on board with no structure

**Fear #4: Future Difficulty Raising Capital**
- **Concern**: "Will Series B investors face problems with this SA structure?"
- **Evidence They Look For**: Standard terms, scalability provisions, clean cap table
- **What Triggers Fear**: Non-standard terms, unusual share classes, complex ownership structures

**Fear #5: Founder Over-Protection**
- **Concern**: "Are founders too defensive? Will they resist reasonable investor oversight?"
- **Evidence They Look For**: Reserved matters, board structure, information rights
- **What Triggers Fear**: No investor board seats, minimal reserved matters, excessive founder control mechanisms
